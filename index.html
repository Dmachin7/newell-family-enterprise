What We Do

Our Services

Professional exterior cleaning for homes and businesses across Spring Hill and surrounding Hernando & Pasco counties.

Exterior House Soft Washing

Safely remove mold, mildew, algae, and grime from siding, stucco, and painted surfaces without high-pressure damage.

Roof Washing

Eliminate black streaks, lichen, and algae from shingles and tile roofs using low-pressure, contractor-approved treatments.

Driveways & Sidewalks

Blast away oil stains, rust, and built-up grime from concrete and paver surfaces — restoring them to like-new condition.

Pool Decks & Cages

Remove algae, mildew, and mineral deposits from pool decks, coping, and screen enclosure frames safely and thoroughly.

Screen Enclosures

Carefully clean screen panels, frames, and sills — removing dirt, pollen, mold, and oxidation without tearing or warping.

Who We Are

A Family Business
Built on Trust

Newell Family Enterprise LLC was founded on a simple belief: your home deserves the same care we'd give our own. As a family-owned and operated business based right here in Spring Hill, FL, we take pride in every job — big or small.

We specialize in exterior soft washing — a gentler, more effective alternative to high-pressure washing that removes contaminants at the source without damaging your home's surfaces. Whether it's a dingy roof, a mossy driveway, or mildew-covered siding, we have the equipment and expertise to handle it.

Our team is fully licensed, bonded, and insured, and we serve both residential and commercial clients throughout Hernando and Pasco counties. When you hire us, you're hiring neighbors who care about your property.
